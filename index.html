introduction

My name is Sigurdur Helgason, I'm a computer science student in the program Research Based Computer Science at Reykjavik University (RU). I'm currently finishing my fourth semester of my BSc. I went to the technical academy in Reykjavik where I got a students degree in computer science. It was there where I figured out that computer science was something I was passionate about.

My focus in Computer Science has mostly been focused on the theoretics of things. The programming languages I would consider myself proficient at would be C/C++, java, python. I mainly use C/C++ in order to get a more low-level understanding on why and how things work the way they do. I've always enjoyed tinkering with things in order to gain knowledge about how they work. So whether it's creating a Back-end webserver to handle multiple connections, making a functional website, or solving complicated programming problems, my goal usually is to know how each piece works.
